You can naviagte to your list by clicking in the top left corner and 
selecting list. From there you can see what is available to be put in the cart

To add something to your cart you can go to the top left corner and select search. 
From there you can search one of the items on the previous page. When the search returns you can
add the item/items to your cart by clicking "add to cart".

After you have added items to your cart and you decide you don't want anything 
anymore you can slect "empty cart" which is located just above "Products" 
on the left side.

You can select the map option and it gives a rough display of a store layout as well as product locations
in our theoretical store. Originally it was to have a shortest path finder function to get all your items, but that function was not completed.
